## Key Insights
- Promotions boosted sales volume but eroded margins (**-5.42% vs +0.26% non-promotion**).  
- Overall profitability was negative (**-0.87%**), signaling unsustainable discounting.  
- Strategic pricing and segmentation are needed to balance growth with profitability.

## Challenges
- Price volatility and margin compression.  
- Competitive pressure forcing discounts.  
- Customer confusion from inconsistent pricing.

## Opportunities
- Premium positioning through stable pricing.  
- Smarter segmentation using historical data.  
- Value communication to reinforce product strength.

## Recommendations
- Limit deep discounts to strategic periods.  
- Benchmark pricing against competitors.  
- Use simulations to forecast pricing impact.  
- Communicate product value clearly to justify pricing.
